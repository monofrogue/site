Welcome to Binyon's Dante!

Dante's Commedia, translated by Laurence Binyon

This was the first translation that really entrapped me, so I was surprised to see that, despite being out of copyright, it wasn't obviously available online. As a result, I have prepared the text and put it here for the enjoyment of all.

There is a PDF version, which is much the best presented, here. However, the online version has the benefit of interactive annotations. You can start at the beginning here.
