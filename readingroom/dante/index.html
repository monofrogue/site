Dante's Commedia, translated by Laurence Binyon

This was the first translation that really enchanted me, so I was surprised to see that, despite being out of copyright, it wasn't obviously available online. As a result, I have prepared the text and put it here for the enjoyment of all.

There is a PDF version, which is much the best presented, here. However, the online version has the benefit of interactive annotations. You can start at the beginning here.

Click here for EPUB and here for the plaintext.

Let me know of any errors you find in the text, no matter how minor.
